About Us

Pokemon Go is a popular game worldwide with a 147 million active users in May since their launch in 2016. In addition, the Pokemon anime has been popular since their first episode in April 1997. Millions of fans not only love the game but also watch the anime of the new generations of Pokemon. There are seven generations in total and about 800 Pokemons. Our goal is to implement complex sql queries and design an optimized Pokemon database. In other words, the project is to develop a user-friendly database platform for Pokemon game players, Pokemon Anime fans, and Pokemon Go players to access the information of each Pokemon and also their opponents.
Our database helps users look up a Pokemon by typing in its name or find the top level Pokemon for a specific type. The basic information of Pokemon, such as Pokedex number, height, weight, female-male ratio, and some other information, will be extracted and displayed with a picture of the Pokemon. Pokemon have a unique Pokedex number and a name, the new Pokemon players may not be familiar with all of them. Even the experienced Pokemon players may not be able to memorize all the information of each Pokemon. In addition, each Pokemon has various different sets of moves and the moves can apply different amounts of damage on different types of Pokemon. Moreover, we will also add a feature for Pokemon Go players to look up the locations where to go to catch the Pokemon they want. Therefore, our database can be used as a game guide for them to get the information and increase their win rate of their battles with other players.
